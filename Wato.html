What are the odds?

This will allow plot the sums of R rolls of D dice with S sides.

Inspiration from https://anydice.com/ and https://megadoug.com/dice-graph/

One entry per line. 1000r10d6s means 1000 rolls of 10 dice with six sides. Dice can be loaded. eg: 1000r10d[1,2,3,4,5,6,6,6]s is a six sided die that has odds of 6 show 3 in 8 and ods of 1 to 5 show 1 in 8 for each.
